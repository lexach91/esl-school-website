Virtual Tour

Take a peek what's inside our school

Our main hall

First thing you see entering our school

We are now looking at our main hall.

Here we have a waiting area for the parents and kids with comfortable seats, reading area, and a couple of play zones.

In the main hall we are going to meet you and your child everyday when you bring him to kindergarten or afterschool classes.

You are always going to feel welcome to sit here and relax, drink some coffee, or maybe even eat a snack while we are taking care of your child.

Our classroom

Let's take a look at a place where children spend most time of their day

Here is one of our classrooms.

Positive and modern classroom environment is really important factor in student's learning. The children should feel safe and secure there.

That's why our classrooms meet all international standards. We want our students feel comfortable all the time.

When children feel safe, secure, and comfortable, it becomes easier for them and for us to concentrate on learning, teaching, and having fun through all the day.

Our canteen

The most delicious part of our tour

Research not once has shown the importance of healthy diet in children's life.

It leads to a better memory, higher grades, and faster information processing. Well nourished kids get enough energy to stay focused through the day.

That's why we pay special attention to our ability to keep our students well and healthy fed. Our kitchen staff consists of professionals only.

So, in our school your child will get not only required knowledge, but healthy and delicious meals as well.

Our art zone

Here we give children a chance to be creative

Integrating art in the learning process is very rewarding. It helps young learners to open themselves.

Art is pushing students to develop creativity, problem-solving skills, motor skills, social skills, and inventiveness.

We help our students to boost their critical thinking, improve academic performance, and explore their interests through the art.

Creating something new is always so exciting for the kids, we love to see them happy and satisfied with their own results.
